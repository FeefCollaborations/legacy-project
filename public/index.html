INITIATIVES

The Veracity Project uses the following criterion to choose which initiatives are supported in each community we travel to:

  • Leveraging local contacts to identify key initiatives in the community
  • Greatest amount of positive impact we could achieve in a month’s time
  • Based on manpower
  • Donations
  • Technical expertise
  • In search of scalable projects where we can help no matter the level of donations.

ABOUT US

The Veracity Project is made up of volunteers who believe in making a tangible positive social impact in an affordable, transparent way.

We are a community of remote professionals that are travelling together for a year, coming from different countries and backgrounds. Our commonality was our commitment to expand our perspectives outside of our existing lives, embarking on this journey to learn more about each other and develop additional compassion for the world.

In the first month together, we recognized a common interest in supporting the local communities that welcomed us for a month at a time. We have a unique opportunity with our diverse skills to form a problem solving community that is focused on creating significant changes in a short amount of time.

The Veracity Project focuses on delivering these changes to the local groups with minimal overhead by tapping into the expertise found in our remote community. You can help us with each initiative through a donation and by sharing our efforts with your social networks. We think it’s important that you know exactly what your money has been used for and the difference it has made. Once completed, we will send you a video to illustrate the impact we were able to make together on these local communities.

Small contributions from a large number of people will help create substantive change in communities around the world. If you share our vision, please join us in this movement.
